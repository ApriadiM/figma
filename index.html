Welcome to Impact Hub Jakarta

Impact Hub Jakarta is a coworking and innovation space that exists to support organisations & entrepreneurs creating sustainable positive impact in Indonesia. We offer programs, events and an inspiring place to work or collaborate with others in the local community, and we are part of a 100+ global network that is passionate about connecting Indonesia to the global entrepreneurial ecosystem.
